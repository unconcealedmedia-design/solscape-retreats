Swiss Alps  ·  Geneva

Geneva
Retreat

July 23–28, 2027  ·  6 Days

Overview

Space to breathe deeply and return to balance

Surrounded by the stillness of the mountains just beyond Geneva, this retreat offers space to breathe deeply, reconnect, and return to balance.

Days begin with sunrise yoga and nourishing breakfasts, followed by movement classes, alpine walks, creative time, and moments of quiet restoration immersed in nature.

Framed by mountain air, forest trails, and sweeping lake views, each evening closes with sunset yoga, wholesome shared meals, and intimate experiences designed to calm the mind and nourish the soul.
