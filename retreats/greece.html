Monemvasia
Retreat

October 4–8, 2026  ·  5 Days

Overview

Between the sea and the timeless landscape

Nestled between the sea and the timeless landscape of the Peloponnese, our Monemvasia retreat invites you into a slower, more nourishing rhythm of life.

Days unfold with sunrise yoga, slow wholesome breakfasts, movement classes, nature hikes, creative exploration, and meaningful shared moments — all set against the backdrop of medieval stone villages and endless blue water.

As the sun sets over the Aegean, we gather again for evening yoga, soulful dinners, and intimate nightly experiences designed to restore, inspire, and reconnect you to yourself.
