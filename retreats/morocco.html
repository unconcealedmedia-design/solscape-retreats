Atlas Mountains  ·  Morocco

Morocco
Retreat

February 1–10, 2027  ·  10 Days

Overview

Reconnect through movement, beauty, and stillness

Set high above the Ourika Valley beneath the Atlas Mountains, our Morocco retreat is an invitation to reconnect through movement, beauty, and stillness.

Days begin with sunrise yoga and slow nourishing breakfasts overlooking the desert landscape, followed by barre, nature walks, creative time, local excursions, and moments of deep rest.

Surrounded by olive groves, Berber villages, and the warm rhythm of Moroccan culture, each evening closes with sunset yoga, candlelit dinners, and intimate gatherings under the stars.
