Our Story

Retreats rooted in movement,
beauty and intentional living

How It Started

Rooted in movement. Shaped by experience. Built with intention.

SolScape began through two different paths that slowly met in the same place.

Samantha is known for her integrative approach to yoga, rooted in over three decades of study and teaching in philosophy, somatic awareness, movement as medicine, and embodied exploration. Her background in rhythmic gymnastics, along with her own experiences of recovery and healing, has deeply shaped her work.

Nada came from a lifelong background in movement through ballet, fitness, and strength training, eventually expanding that journey into a broader exploration of wellness, balance, and what movement can become through different stages of life.

What brought them together was a shared appreciation for retreats that feel both grounding and intentional — experiences where movement, environment, and meaningful connection come together naturally.

Together, they began creating the kinds of retreats they themselves are drawn to: thoughtfully curated experiences rooted in movement, held within beautiful settings that encourage presence, rest, clarity, and reconnection.

Over the past three years, SolScape has moved through Egypt, Greece, Tuscany, and Spain — with each retreat shaping the next chapter of what the experience continues to become.

Our Philosophy

"Welcome to SolScape Retreats — where movement, adventure, and soulful living come together."

We believe in the power of yoga and barre to ground and energize, in adventure to awaken the spirit, and in soulful moments that create lasting transformation. Whether you're seeking deep relaxation, self-discovery, or a journey beyond the ordinary, we craft experiences that nourish the mind, body, and soul.

Join us as we explore, move, and connect — one retreat at a time.

The Founders

Meet Nada & Samantha

Samantha

Founder of One Yoga Academy | Integrative Yoga & Mind–Body Educator

Samantha is the founder of One Yoga Academy and an integrative mind–body educator whose work is rooted in over three decades of immersive practice, study, and teaching.

With a background spanning rhythmic gymnastics, yoga philosophy, contemplative inquiry, somatic exploration, and transpersonal psychology, her approach blends movement, breath, and embodied awareness into practices that support presence, healing, and deeper connection to self.

Alongside a Master's degree in Fine Art and Sculpture, Samantha's teaching is shaped by both structure and creativity — guiding people beyond prescribed forms and into a more intuitive and lived relationship with the body, mind, and inner experience.

Through SolScape, she shares thoughtfully curated retreat experiences alongside her dear friend Nada, bringing together their complementary strengths and shared love for meaningful connection, beauty, presence, and experiences that leave a lasting imprint long after the retreat ends.

@oneyogasamantha

Nada

Barre Instructor & Movement Educator

Nada is a barre instructor and movement educator whose path has moved through fitness, business, and creative entrepreneurship, with movement remaining the constant throughout it all.

From ballet and strength training to barre, her work is shaped by years of personal practice and a growing interest in how women move, adapt, and reconnect with themselves through different stages of life.

Currently continuing her education through NASM in Women's Fitness and Behavior Change, Nada brings a grounded and thoughtful approach to movement — one rooted not in perfection, but in feeling stronger, more balanced, and more connected to yourself.

Through SolScape, she helps create thoughtfully curated retreats centered around movement, meaningful experiences, beautiful surroundings, and genuine connection.

@forbarre
